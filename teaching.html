Teaching

In my career, I've provided student-centered semester-long university-level instruction, as well as shorter format research workshops and sessions. On this page, you will find information about this central aspect of my work through my teaching philosophy and my teaching experience.

Philosophy

My teaching philosophy and approach to instruction is focused on holistic, experiential education within an inclusive classroom.

Experience

Since 2014, I have taught credit-bearing semester-long courses and completed faculty development. In my work as a librarian, I also teach short format lectures and workshops on a variety of scholarly research topics in STEM and science communication.
